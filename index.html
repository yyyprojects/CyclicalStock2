The Economic Cycle is Their Engine

The performance of cyclical stocks follows the four phases of the U.S. economic cycle. They don't just mirror the economy; they often amplify its movements.

Cyclical vs. Defensive Stocks

The opposite of a cyclical stock is a defensive stock. Understanding their differences in volatility is key for investors.

Volatility is often measured by Beta. A Beta above 1.0 means a stock is more volatile than the overall market. A Beta below 1.0 means it's less volatile.

  • üöÄ
    Cyclical Stocks: High Beta. They offer higher potential returns but come with greater risk.

    Defensive Stocks: Low Beta. They provide stability and consistent dividends, as they sell essential goods like food and utilities.
